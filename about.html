I grew up in North Florida and have had the priviledge of living in NYC for many years. While working in NYC and attending Hunter College, I took an urban and population geography class which highlighted my daily, lived experiences. From there I took another geography class and another...and appearantly I can't stop...Now a second year Ph.D. student in Geography at UW-Madison, my research interests include political geography, feminist methodology, and people-environment relationships.
